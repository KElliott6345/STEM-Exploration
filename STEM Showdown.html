Overview

One of the only STEM trivia competitions during the fall semester, STEM Showdown offers high schoolers the chance to compete against other like-minded individuals and apply real world STEM knowledge in a fast-paced environment. Represent your high school, compete for glory, and win cash prizes!

Competition Structure


Round 1: Preliminary

The first round of this competition is the preliminary cutoff. Members will log on to the competition portal with their usernames and the top 100 competitors will move on to the next round.

Round 2: Tier II Trivia

the Tier II Trivia round throws the top 100 competitors from the previous round for a loop...was the first round just a trap to get everyone nice and comfortable? Same competition portal but question difficulty becomes much higher.

Round 3: Showdown

The top 20 competitors will be placed in a randomly generated matchup sequence to face off against the other teams. In some epic battles, only two shall remain, and one shall claim victory!
