Introducing Nikhil Kumar!

Nikhil Kumar is the founder and CEO of STEM Exploratios. He loves pushing his boundaries and providing opportunity to the people in his community. Nikhil founded STEM Explorations to provide opportunity to under-epresented population and to give high school students the experience he wishes he had. He is a scholar, buisness man, an athlete, and a great friend. He loves connecting with his fellow STEM Exploration members, so shoot him up anytime!
