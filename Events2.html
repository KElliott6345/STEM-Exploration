STEM Showdown

The STEM Showdown is our premium competition for STEM enthusiasts. Combining advanced knowledge in the fields of biology, physics, chemistry, and mathematics this trivia competition is bound to excite anyone who participates!

STEMChats

STEMChats is a program that allows aspiring youth to learn from professionals in STEM fields about the various experiences, challenges, and benefits of pursuing a career in their field. It also allows SE members to meet new mentors and form connections that will help them kickstart their career.

Innovation Challenge

Do you like getting your hands dirty and innovating by doing? If you said yes, then this is the perfect challenge for you. The STEM Explorations Innovation Challenge allows you to embrace your creative side to tackle problems facing our world today.
